Use Case of Menstrual Products

Menstrual products such as sanitary pads, tampons, and menstrual cups are essential for maintaining hygiene and dignity during menstruation. These products help prevent infections, reduce discomfort, and support participation in daily activities, school, and work.

Menstrual Product Benefits

Sanitary Pads

Easy to use, widely available, and great for beginners. Disposable and hygienic option for everyday comfort.

Menstrual Cups

Eco-friendly, reusable for years, and cost-effective. Can be worn for up to 12 hours safely.

Tampons

Discreet, excellent for physical activities, and available in various absorbencies for different flow levels.

Reusable Cloth Pads

Environmentally sustainable, reusable for years, gentle on skin, and customizable for comfort.

Period Panties

Built-in absorbency, comfortable all-day wear, reusable, and ideal for light to medium flow days.
